## Decided (do not re-litigate)

- **Reviews lack `workspace_id`.** Do not denormalize workspace onto review rows
  in this tier; `listForWorkspace` keeps the WorkUnit join. A future promotion of
  `workspace_id` onto review (or a composite index strategy) is a separate ADR
  change.
- **Host-scoped full reads are correct** for workspaces, workers, sessions ‚Äî they
  are host registries, not workspace-scoped indexes.
- **Lease `--holder` filtering stays client-side** (CLI); domain `list` only needs
  workspace scope.
- **Idempotent additive DDL** (no migration framework) is enough for this tier's
  promoted columns; a real `@effect/sql` migrator remains future work.
